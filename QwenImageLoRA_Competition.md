## 🛤️ Competition Tracks

### Track 1: AI for Production
*Build LoRAs for real-world creativity and business use.*
*   **Brand Style Transfer:** Adapt brand visual identities to new products or campaigns.
*   **Industry-Specific Visuals:** High-accuracy LoRAs for architecture, fashion, interior design, etc.
*   **Single/Few-Shot Style Driving:** Master niche aesthetics with minimal high-quality data.
*   **Design Efficiency Tools:** Icons, UI elements, or stock-quality assets for professional workflows.

### Track 2: AI for Good
*Create LoRAs that empower communities, preserve culture, or support accessibility.*
*   **Revive Endangered Crafts:** Generate new patterns based on UNESCO heritage arts (e.g., batik, weaving).
*   **Tactile Graphics for the Visually Impaired:** Convert complex images into bold-outline, fill-free line art for braille printers.
*   **Autism-Friendly Illustrations:** Calm, clear, and predictable visuals for personalized storybooks in special education.
*   **Cultural & Language Preservation:** Pair local scripts with imagery for bilingual educational content.

## ⚖️ Essential Rules
1.  **Base Model:** You must use any version of **Qwen-Image** as the base model.
2.  **Submission Limit:** Maximum of one entry per track per participant.
3.  **Method:** **LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation) ONLY**. Full-parameter fine-tuning is prohibited.
4.  **Data Compliance:** Training data must be legally sourced; no unauthorized use of copyrighted works.
5.  **Platform Choice:** Training on **Civision (ModelScope)** is encouraged for the best experience, but local training is also permitted. Final LoRA must be uploaded to ModelScope with the link submitted.
